Florin Pop

Florin pop is a awesome #Developer👨🏼‍💻. click here below to see more of his great accomplishments

It all started in 2013 when a good friend of Florin introduced him to web development. They started learning HTML, and quickly after He got into CSS, at that point he was mind blown by it's power!

A few months later, He created a Freelancer account and started bidding on different jobs there. Some of the clients liked his work so he got himself few long-time gigs.

Florin started learning jQuery to enhance his "web powers", but after a while, realizing that jQuery is built on top of JavaScript, he dropped it and started learning Vanilla JS, and when he felt that he understood what is going on under the hood, he picked ReactJS to help him on the long run on the front-end. He also added NodeJS (back-end) to his skills palette so he could provide Full-Stack JavaScript services.
